Visualizing Boston's Bike Sharing System

The bluebikes, formerly named Hubway Bikes, is Boston's public bike sharing program. Since 2012, this system has increased access to various parts of Boston through active and affordable means of transportation. The data shows trip histories for the month of December 2018 throughout the neighborhoods of Boston, Somerville, Brookline, and Cambridge. This visualization explores the expanding network of bluebike stations and usage by trip duration, gender, membership, and age. - Diana Morel
